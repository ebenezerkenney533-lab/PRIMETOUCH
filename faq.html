Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our painting and restoration process.

How long does a full respray take?

Typically, a full respray takes between 7 to 14 days. This depends on the amount of prep work, dent repairs, and drying time required for a showroom finish.

Can you match my car's original color exactly?

Yes. We use a digital spectrometer and factory paint codes to ensure a 100% color match, even for older or faded paint.

Do you offer a warranty on the paintwork?

Absolutely. We provide a 3-year warranty against peeling or fading, giving you peace of mind that the finish will last.

How soon can I wash my car after it's been sprayed?

You can wash it with water after 48 hours, but we recommend waiting 30 days before using high-pressure jet washes or wax to allow the paint to fully cure.

Do I need to bring the car in for a quote?

While photos help for a rough estimate, we prefer seeing the vehicle in person to give you an accurate, no-obligation quote.
